Biography

Dr. Sarah Taylor is a highly experienced neurosurgeon with over 18 years of practice in diagnosing and treating complex neurological conditions. She completed her MBBS with distinction and went on to earn her PHD in Neurosurgery from the prestigious University of Mediserv.

Her areas of specialization include brain tumor surgery, spinal cord interventions, and minimally invasive neurosurgical techniques. Dr. Taylor is known for her patient-centered approach, ensuring each individual receives personalized, compassionate care throughout their treatment journey.

She has published numerous peer-reviewed research papers in international medical journals and regularly participates in global neurology conferences, keeping her practice at the forefront of medical innovation.
